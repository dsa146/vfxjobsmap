— How to Use
  • Search by studio, role, or city in the top bar
  • Click a pin on the map to see all jobs at that location
  • Click a job card in the feed to open full details
  • Discipline chips filter by department — multi-select supported
  • Software chips match against job requirements — multi-select supported
  • Apply Now in the job drawer links to the original posting
  • View Studio shows all open roles at that studio
  • Status reflects how recently a job was posted — New means today
  • Featured jobs are highlighted in cyan and appear first in the feed
